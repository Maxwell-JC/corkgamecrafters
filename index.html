Introduction

View Games

Heya!! My name is Maxwell and I make Board Games, Card Games and Tabletop Games in Cork.

I try to make everything free to download with physical copies made from 100% upcycled / recyclable materials to be sold at markets


Craft Events

View Events

Whenever I have the time, I always try to go to Irish game markets & conventions.

At events, me and my friends (who are the best!!) hand make board games, card games & tabletop games to sell and raise money for Transgender Equality Network Ireland


Non - Profit

View T.E.N.I

All profits that I get from selling my games go directly to Transgender Equality Network Ireland.

"TENI" are a non – profit organization advocating for Transgender rights and healthcare in Ireland.


End Goals

View Linktree

I started Cork Game Crafters because I love making my silly little games. I think down the line, I would adore having my work in LGBTQ+ friendly spaces across the country.

There’s a few cafes, clubs and bars that I plan on making donations to in Cork and I’ll see how well that goes first before I start popping up to Dublin to hand out free decks of cards.
